My Favorite Apple Pie

If you don't know how to cook (my case), then this apple pie recipe is for you! The recipe is very simple, requires a minimum of ingredients and no fuss with the dough!

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1.5 tablespoon baking powder
  • 4 apples
  • ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 glass of wine (optional)

Directions:

Step 1

Beat eggs with sugar with a mixer.

Step 2

Add baking powder and flour, beat thoroughly.

Step 3

Peel and core apples, then thinly slice.

Step 4

Place sliced apples into a form greased with butter. Sprinkle with cinnamon if desired.

Step 5

Slowly and gently pour our mixture over sliced apples. The mixture must be evently distributed.

Step 6

Preheat the oven to 355 degrees F (180 degrees C).

Step 7

Bake in the preheated oven for 40 minutes. Check the readness of the pie with a toothpick. The toothpick inserted in the center should come out mostly clean.

Step 8

Let the pie cool down a bit. Turn the pie over so the apple layer is on top. Sprinckle with powdered sugar.

Delicious apple pie is ready! Bon appetit!

You may have noticed that we did not use one important ingredient - wine. Yes, you thought right! If you drink a glass of wine before you start cooking, the process will go much more fun!
