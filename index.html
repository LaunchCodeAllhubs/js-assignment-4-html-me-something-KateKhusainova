My Favorite Apple Pie

If you don't know how to cook (my case), then this apple pie recipe is for you! The recipe is very simple, requires a minimum of ingredients and no fuss with the dough!

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1.5 tablespoon baking powder
  • 4 apples
  • ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 glass of wine (optional)

Directions:

Step 1

Beat eggs with sugar with a mixer.

Step 2

Add baking powder and flour, beat thoroughly.

Step 3

Peel and core apples, then thinly slice.

Step 4

Place sliced apples into a form greased with butter. Sprinkle with cinnamon if desired.

Step 5

Slowly and gently pour our mixture over sliced apples. The mixture must be evenly distributed.

Step 6

Preheat the oven to 355 degrees F (180 degrees C).

Step 7

Bake in the preheated oven for 40 minutes. Check the readiness of the pie with a toothpick. The toothpick inserted in the center should come out mostly clean.

Step 8

Let the pie cool down a bit. Turn the pie over so the apple layer is on top. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
